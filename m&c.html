Mac & Cheese

Servings: 4   Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients:












Preparation:

  1. Boil water for pasta, salt generously, and cook for 8 minutes.
  2. While pasta is cooking, melt butter in medium saucepan with olive oil. Add the flour and stir, cooking for about a minute.
  3. Add in milk and cook until hot, then add in cheese and stir until melted.
  4. Season with salt, nutmeg, and paprika.
  5. Serve with crunchy topping and chopped green onion.
